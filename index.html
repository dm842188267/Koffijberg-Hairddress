EXTENTIONS & FILLERS


Would you like to have a full and lush hairstyle, create length for an updo, or change to a different color? Are you hesitant to make a permanent change? Using Balmain hair extensions, we can make these dreams come true. Hair extensions are hair pieces made of real or synthetic hair, which can be placed between your own, natural hair. As a result, your hair becomes longer and/or fuller. They last 3 months. You can add just a few extensions or the so-called Longueur+ hair pieces, which equal 18 extensions. Your hair remains easy to style: creating curls, blow-drying, everything is possible. Having a ponytail or a hair piece for just one night only is also possible. To discuss the possibilities and to receive a personalized advice, you can make an appointment in our salon. This appointment is both informal and free of charge.
